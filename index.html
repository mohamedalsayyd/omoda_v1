OMODA will present a new futuristic crossover at Blazar

September 1, 2022

In September 2022, the OMODA brand will introduce a new fastback crossover . The car will become an art object at one of the main events of the year - the blazar young contemporary art fair, which will be held from September 14 to 18 at the Museum of Moscow.

To create an art installation, blazar curators chose three young artists: Sasha Puchkova, Maxim Emelyanov and Nikita Khodak. Each of them created their own unique design sketch especially for OMODA . As a result of voting in the official OMODA community, Maxim Yemelyanov won.

When creating the sketch, the artist used machine learning and an artificial neural network. The main idea of ​​the work is to create an ideal world, the matrix in which creativity and futurism are encrypted. Maxim Emelyanov was inspired by the concept of the OMODA brand - "Art in motion" - art in motion.

The design will be applied to the car, and the new OMODA crossover will appear before the general public as an art object, a symbol of the synthesis of art and modern technology.

Blazar Fair is the most important event of the year in the art world. Galleries, creative associations and young independent artists present their vision of the present and future from a new angle.

More details in the official OMODA Telegram channels:

About OMODA brand



OMODA is a new automotive brand in the high-middle segment. The DNA of the brand is innovative technological solutions and futuristic design. The OMODA crossover is the first model to be presented in Russia this fall. The concept of OMODA is "Art in Motion", which is based on an avant-garde approach to color and form. An international team led by the brand's chief designer Steve Hume worked on the design of the car.

About blazar fair



blazar is a fair for young contemporary art, a satellite of the Moscow international fair of contemporary art cosmoscow, has been held since 2020. blazar members are not only galleries, but also early-career independent artists working outside of gallery structures, as well as arts associations, educational institutions and non-profit institutions. Assistance with the selection of works at the fair is provided by mediators trained by the blazar team. blazar is accompanied by a lecture program, with discussions by artists, curators, patrons and art managers, a performance program, children's excursions, workshops and a parallel program outside the fair space.

About the artist Maxim Emelyanov



Maxim Emelyanov works at the intersection of issues that stand out in modern graphic art related to the participation of artificial neural networks in the collection and analytics of visual information. It uses generative tools such as the Deep Dream algorithm, some kinds of GAN neural networks, text-to-image models.

The elegant interior of the OMODA crossover

August 24, 2022

The OMODA brand officially presents the interior of the crossover in Russia. The model will go on sale this autumn and will be available at the first stage in three trim levels, 13 color combinations and one powertrain - a 1.5T turbocharged gasoline engine with 147 hp. in combination with a CVT25 gearbox.

The OMODA has a sleek interior that matches the car's futuristic look. The interior will be available in black and brown. The front seats are made according to the canons of sports cars - a high one-piece headrest, advanced lateral support for seat cushions, perforated high-quality eco-leather. The driver's seat is equipped with electric adjustments in six directions, the front passenger seat in four.

The instrument panel and the infotainment complex screen are presented in the form of dual LCD screens with a diagonal of 10.25 inches each and an HD resolution of 1920x720 pixels. The center display allows you to connect an iOS or Android smartphone, displaying the content of the device, as well as data provided by the 360°AVM surround view system, which is activated automatically when driving at a speed of less than 20 km/h. The choice of graphic design of the digital on-board computer is provided, depending on the control mode. The multimedia system gives access to the sound field settings in the cabin, personalization of the driver assistants, adjusting the opening height of the rear tailgate, access to the data of the built-in registrar and choosing the color of the atmospheric interior lighting.

On the side of the console are an electronic gear selector, electric parking brake (EPB) and automatic (AutoHold) buttons, control mode switching, keyless entry to the car and engine start, quick access to the surround view system and heated seat control buttons. The designers also provide a place to place the key, as well as a niche for a smartphone, where you can charge it wirelessly. There was also a place for two full-fledged coasters. In a niche under the console, the designers ergonomically placed a 12-volt outlet and two USBs, including type-C.

The OMODA crossover is equipped with a winter package that includes heated windshields and rear windows, exterior mirrors, front wiper nozzles, steering wheel and seats. Intelligent dual-zone climate control creates a comfortable atmosphere and keeps the air clean in the cabin. Additional confidence in air quality is provided by the high reliability N95 cabin filter, which belongs to the FFP2 protection class.

The multifunction leather steering wheel allows you to control the functions of the car without taking your hands off the car and without taking your eyes off the wheel. It is worth noting that the OMODA provides a package of ADAS driver assistance systems for comfortable trips, which includes: adaptive cruise control (ACC), traffic jam assistance (TJA / ICA), forward collision warning system (FCW), system Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB), Lane Departure Warning (LDW), Lane Keeping Assist (LDP), Emergency Lane Keeping Assist (ELK), Rear Cross Collision Warning (RCTW) and Automatic Low/High Beam Switching (IHC) .

Information about trim levels and pricing policy will be presented in the next series of official releases of the OMODA brand.

Powertrain

The OMODA model will be equipped with a 1.5T high-performance gasoline turbocharged engine with a capacity of 147 hp. with a torque of 210 Nm, which is achieved in the crankshaft speed range from 1750 to 4000 rpm, and at least 80% of its maximum value is available throughout the entire engine operating range (from 1350 to "cut-off" at 5500 rpm). This "elasticity" improves dynamics and is achieved through the use of a Garrett (Honeywell) turbocharger and BorgWarner's dual-phase adjustment of the intake and exhaust camshafts.

The engine is paired with a reliable and durable CVT25 transmission, which was ranked among the best in the "Top 10 Best Transmissions in the World" ranking, compiled by the Lopal Cup Automotive Evaluation Academy expert community last year. The CVT25 transmission was recognized as the best along with the Hydra-Matic 10L80 (Cadillac CT6), ZF 8HP (BMW 5), DQ400e (Volkswagen), TCT9 (Mercedes) and others. It integrates a number of advanced technologies, such as an independent hydraulic system, a steel belt from Bosch that improves transmission efficiency up to 92.5%, a high-efficiency flow-controlled vane pump with low viscosity oil, which has excellent performance in terms of reliability, fuel consumption, dynamic response and low noise.

The CVT25 has a maximum torque of 250 Nm, the gear ratio reaches 7.07, thereby expanding the rpm selection range to achieve better fuel economy and better acceleration performance. Up to 97% transmission efficiency also minimizes power consumption. This allows you to enjoy a trip with low fuel consumption and a high level of comfort.

Such a power unit provides acceleration of the OMODA fastback crossover to 100 km / h in 9.9 seconds, the maximum speed is 195 km / h. Fuel consumption for every 100 kilometers is 5.8 liters in suburban mode, 7.1 liters in mixed mode and 9.5 liters in city mode. Eco / Normal / Sport modes are available to the driver, depending on the purpose and individual preferences. The manufacturer allows the use of gasoline with a 92-octane rating.

Dimensions and suspension

The dimensions of the OMODA crossover, built on the T1X modular platform, are: length - 4400 mm, width - 1830 mm, height - 1588 mm. Thanks to the wheelbase of 2630 mm, the interior of the car turned out to be spacious and roomy. The volume of the luggage compartment is 378 liters. The ground clearance is 190 mm, which provides good cross-country ability on light off-road and comfortable parking at high curbs.

Front suspension type McPherson and rear - semi-independent, spring, with hydraulic telescopic shock absorbers and anti-roll bar.

OMODA 5 rated Five-stars by Euro NCAP, Highlighting its Excellent Safety Quality

December 8, 2022

On December 7, Euro NCAP released the latest safety test results. OMODA 5 passed the professional and most rigorous safety evaluation, with the highest evaluation of five-star safety by virtue of its outstanding performance in four areas, including adult occupant, child occupant, vulnerable road users and safety assist.

OMODA 5's success in the most rigorous Euro NCAP test is attributable to its emphasis on users' interests and its own highest pursuit of quality. The cross-style SUV has catered to the key demands of global consumers from the R&D and design stage. It has not only made great advancements in body structure, safety configuration and other aspects, but also carried out adaptive development verification in different environments and road conditions around the world.

OMODA 5 adopts a body with high-strength steel as the main structure, with steel accounting for 78%. It can withstand pressures up to 1800Mpa. Equipped with the above ultra-high strength materials, the cage body has been designed and manufactured to maintain its overall structure without deformation in case of accidents. As for the cabin, OMODA 5 is equipped with 7 airbags including for the driver and passenger seats, side airbag, side curtain and middle airbag, which can provide all-round safety protection for occupants in the event of a collision.

OMODA 5’s cutting-edge intelligent technology is the latest in a line of continuous progress, bringing the ultimate driving and riding safety experience. Specifically, The OMODA 5 is equipped with an advanced intelligent driving assistance system, including the ADAS system with 16 functions, such as ACC adaptive cruise, to protect drivers and passengers.

OMODA 5 successfully passed the Euro NCAP safety crash test with a evaluation of the full five stars, fully interpreting the product concept of "integrating safety in its products". It also manifests that, by virtue of the impressive safety performance, OMODA 5 will step onto the international stage with a new attitude. Up to now, OMODA 5 has been launched in Kuwait, Israel and several other markets. With the continuous global certification of its quality, the OMODA brand will win the deeper trust of users in the process of globalization in the future.
